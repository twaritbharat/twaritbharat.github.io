About Us

Twarit Bharat is an organization initiated by the students of IIT Roorkee.

We are the group of like-minded people working for outcome based work for welfare of rural India. The expansion of Twarit is ‘Technocrats With Application in Rural India & Towns’. The meaning is self-explanatory i.e. Bringing Technocrats/Technology to The Villages where The Real Problems lies and their Rectification may add much more push towards Growth of India.

We are a team of dedicated people from various fields working together for integrated development in rural India and small towns.
Twarit Bharat aims for India as a nation where villages and small towns are self-sufficient and have enough economic strength.

Our Activities

Rural Education

Quality and access to education is the major concern in rural schools as there are fewer committed teachers, lack of proper text books and learning material in the schools. Our volunteers usually go to the villages on weekends.We try to know their week areas and teach them accordingly. Our volunteers also provide career guidance and counselling to the students.

Skill Development

To provide opportunities to the rural and economically underprivileged people through Education, Training, Skill & Enterprise Development.Our major area of working is "Handicraft Making" which is made by the village ladies and is sold in market by conducting exibhitions or other means with the tag of "Beladi Handicrafts".We are working in village Beladi Salhapur in district Haridwar.

Govt. Welfare Schemes

Rural Development in general and poverty alleviation and employment generation in particular, are the biggest challenges before policy makers of India. Despite launching numerous programmes and schemes for poverty alleviation and employment generation, the overall picture of rural Indian continues to decline.Our Volunteers takes feedback to local Govt. implementation body in order to ensure proper delivery of Govt. Welfare Schemes.

Our Skill development program includes training of people of the Village/town in which we are working by some trained professionals. Skill development program are in both service and manufacturing sectors. It also includes the marketing of manufactured product by the after skill teaching is being done. Also the arrangements of Job & placements for them who are trained under service skill development.

On-Going Projects

Handicraft Training & Manufacturing

We are training the unemployed women from 35 families of village Beladi near Roorkee Disst.:Haridwar(Uttrakhand). The Trainer is a President-Prize winner in the field of handicraft. After the completion of training period i.e. 3 months we’ll make arrangements of selling/marketing/branding of the manufactured product. We are in touch with NSDC(National Skill Development Corporation) for scaling up the training and manufacturing of handicrafts.

Electrician Training Program

We are planning of training around 30-40 young guys of a village in Rajasthan.The demand for electrician will be identified by our research division and the trained electrician will be placed accordingly. We have collaboration with a Jaipur based company for the training.Our research division finds technical solutions for the placements of trained skilled people e.g. Mobile App based electrician call model.
